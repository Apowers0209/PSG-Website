SCA Quick Reference

Service Contract Act — Key Numbers

The figures every federal IT staffing firm and prime contractor should know. PSG verifies all of these before accepting any position.

Current Rate

H&W Fringe — $5.55/hr

Current SCA Health & Welfare fringe benefit rate effective July 7, 2025. Required on all covered federal service contracts. Paid in addition to the base wage — not instead of it. Updated annually by DOL.

Annual Hours

2,080 Paid / 1,912 Billable

Standard annual hours for a full-time SCA worker. 2,080 paid hours (52 weeks x 40 hours). 1,912 billable hours after subtracting 88 holiday hours and 80 vacation hours. Used in all PSG pricing calculations.

Paid Leave

11 Holidays + 10 Days PTO

SCA requires 11 paid federal holidays annually. Most VA contracts also require 10 days (80 hours) paid vacation. EO 13706 mandates 1 hour of paid sick leave per 30 hours worked, up to 56 hours per year.

Important: Wage Determination rates vary by occupation and locality. Always pull the current WD from SAM.gov for the specific work location before pricing or accepting any position. WDs change annually and stale rates create compliance risk. PSG pulls a fresh WD for every position before accepting.
